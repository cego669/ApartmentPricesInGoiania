### ðŸŒŸ Overview

The model used to predict the price of apartments in GoiÃ¢nia was trained based on the **main characteristics of the apartment** (number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, total area, floor, etc.) as well as additional characteristics of the ad, such as the **main image** and the **full description** of the advertiser. The **mean percentage error** of the model on the data separated for testing was **13.64%**.
